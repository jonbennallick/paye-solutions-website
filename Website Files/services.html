Fully Managed Payroll Service

“It’s like having your own in-house payroll team.”

What's Included

Everything you need to run your payroll, handled by us.

Payroll Calculations

Tax and National Insurance computations, statutory payments (SSP, SMP, SAP, SPP), tax credits, student loan deductions, and attachment of earnings orders, all calculated accurately every pay run.

Payslips & Documentation

Payslips delivered by email, P45s for leavers, and management and accounting reports, keeping you and your employees fully informed.

Year-End Processing

P60s prepared and distributed, year-end returns filed with HMRC, and new tax year setup, all handled so you don't have to worry about deadlines.

Pension Contributions

Auto-enrolment assessment, pension contribution calculations, and reporting to your chosen provider as part of your regular payroll processing.

Payrolled Benefits in Kind (P11Ds)

Report benefits through payroll in real time, simplifying year-end reporting and replacing traditional P11D submissions.

Monthly BACS Provision

Secure, scheduled BACS payments for salaries and HMRC liabilities, handled directly from your payroll run.
